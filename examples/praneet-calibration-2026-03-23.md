# Praneet Calibration Capture (2026-03-23)

## Captured preferences
- Optimization: clarity/trust first, then uniqueness, then learning velocity
- Scope default: force a two-option decision when uncertain
- PMF evidence: qualitative is acceptable early; fit claims remain assumptions without mixed evidence
- Conflict handling: present interview and behavioral views side-by-side
- UX quality bar: no critical confusion
- Risk posture: balanced
- Communication: structured and calm
- Design expression: context-driven with rationale
- Ask policy: at key strategy gates and before irreversible recommendations
- Non-negotiables: no dark patterns; no vanity metrics over user value
- Artifact style: hybrid checklist + memo + scorecard
- Decision logs: both daily and milestone
